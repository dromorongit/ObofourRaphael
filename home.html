Obofour Raphael

Silvanus Raphael Oduro Dickson, popularly known as Obofour Raphael, was born on April 10th in Brescia, in the Lombardy region of Italy. Although born abroad, he was raised in Dansoman, Accra, Ghana, where his passion for music was nurtured alongside his educational journey.
